Darjeeling, often referred to as the "Queen of the Hills," is a charming hill station nestled in the foothills of the eastern Himalayas in West Bengal, India. Renowned for its breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, including the majestic Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling offers a serene escape from the bustle of city life. Its cool climate, lush tea gardens, and colonial-era architecture add to its timeless appeal. The famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers a nostalgic ride through misty mountains and picturesque valleys. Whether you're sipping a cup of world-famous Darjeeling tea or strolling along the Mall Road, the town's unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness leaves a lasting impression.
